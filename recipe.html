No-Bake Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 200g digestive biscuits
  • 100g melted butter
  • 300g cream cheese
  • 200ml whipping cream
  • 100g powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Crush the biscuits and mix with melted butter. Press into a pan to form the base.
  2. Beat cream cheese, sugar, vanilla, and cream until smooth.
  3. Pour onto base and chill 4+ hours. Serve cold.

Red Velvet Cake

Ingredients:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 tbsp red food coloring

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C. Grease and flour two 8-inch pans.
  2. Mix dry & wet ingredients separately, then combine.
  3. Pour into pans. Bake 30–35 mins. Let cool & frost.

Tiramisu Cake

Ingredients:

  • 250g mascarpone cheese
  • 3 eggs (separated)
  • 100g sugar
  • 1 cup strong coffee
  • 1 pack ladyfinger biscuits
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

Instructions:

  1. Whisk yolks with sugar & mascarpone. Fold in whipped egg whites.
  2. Dip biscuits in coffee. Layer with cream. Repeat.
  3. Chill 4–6 hours. Dust with cocoa before serving.
